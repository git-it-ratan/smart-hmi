01 / The Problem

Alarm overload kills.
The data proves it.

Every major industrial disaster of the past 40 years has one thing in common: operators couldn't distinguish the signal from the noise.

No alarm grouping

01B / How Cascades Work

One failure. Four alarms.
Zero context.

This is how a single pump trip creates an alarm flood in a water treatment plant — and how our system handles it differently.

// PUMP P1 FAILURE — CASCADE SEQUENCE — WATER TREATMENT PLANT
1
Pump P1 motor overloads and trips
Motor temperature rises from 65°C to 87°C. P1 automatically shuts down. Backup pump P2 is in standby.
ALM-0042: P1_MOTOR_TEMP HIGH — 87.3°C — CRITICAL
2
Inlet flow rate collapses
With P1 down, flow drops from 100 L/min to 18 L/min within 40 seconds. A second alarm fires independently.
ALM-0088: FLOW_INLET LOW — 18 L/min — HIGH
3
Inlet tank level begins falling
Without inflow, the inlet tank drains. Level drops below 20% threshold. A third alarm — entirely separate in the traditional list.
ALM-0103: TANK_INLET LEVEL LOW — 17% — HIGH
4
System pressure drops
Reduced flow causes pressure to fall below 1 bar. Fourth alarm fires. Operator now sees 4 critical alerts with no link between them.
ALM-0156: PRESSURE LOW — 0.7 bar — CRITICAL
✓
OperatorAI groups all 4 into ONE incident
"Critical: Pump P1 failure at 14:32. Motor temperature triggered cascade across flow, tank level, and pressure sensors. Root cause: P1 trip. Backup pump P2 ready to start. ETA to critical tank level: 8 minutes."

03 / How It Works

Three layers of intelligence.

No black-box ML. A transparent, explainable system that works exactly the way judges can follow in real time.

01
Simulation Engine
10 sensors, 3 failure scenarios, JavaScript timers with realistic propagation delays. A pump trip takes 40 seconds to cascade — exactly like a real plant.
02
Scoring Algorithm
Rule-based incident scoring: severity level, cascade count, chattering detection, trend direction, and urgency escalation. Transparent, debuggable, and ISA-18.2 aligned.
03
Role Engine + AI
Three role views (Operator, Engineer, Supervisor) with tailored guidance. Optional LLM narration converts structured incident JSON into plain-English summaries.
03B / Scoring Algorithm

Every score has a reason.

Explainability beats black-box accuracy in a 5-minute demo. Judges can follow every number.

Factor Score Contribution Why It Matters
Alarm severity level Critical +40 / High +25 Direct ISA-18.2 compliance
Cascade count +8 per linked component Detects root-cause relationships
Chattering detection +15 if 3+ fires in 10 min Filters nuisance alarms automatically
Trend direction +12 if worsening Proactive — fires before threshold breach
Time unacknowledged +5 every 2 minutes Urgency escalation for ignored incidents
Role relevance filter Show / hide per role Reduces irrelevant noise per person
