ABB Accelerator 2026 · Theme 1: Next-Gen Control System Interface
"Not a better dashboard. An operator copilot."
OperatorAI transforms industrial alarm floods into calm, guided, role-specific action — using an intelligent incident grouping engine and AI narration to help operators see what matters before it becomes a crisis.
Built for the ABB Accelerator 2026 pan-India innovation challenge, Bengaluru, June 25 2026.

The Problem:
Every major industrial disaster of the past 40 years shares one common thread: operators couldn't distinguish the signal from the noise.

Traditional vs Smart Mode Toggle: The centerpiece of the demo. One click collapses 47 scattered alarms into a single prioritised Incident Card — judges see the transformation in real time.

Cascading Failure Simulation: A JavaScript-driven simulator models a realistic water treatment plant with 10 sensors and 3 failure scenarios. When Pump P1 trips, flow, tank level, and pressure degrade with real propagation delays — exactly as they would in a physical plant.
Incident Grouping Engine: Related alarms are clustered into a single Incident Card with a severity score, root cause hint, and timeline. No ML — a transparent, rule-based scoring algorithm that judges can follow live.

Role-Based Guidance (3 Roles): 
The same incident surfaces completely different information depending on who is logged in:
Operator → Immediate action steps ("Start backup pump P2 immediately")
Engineer → Root cause analysis and maintenance checklist
Supervisor → Business impact summary and ETA to recovery

AI Shift Log Sidebar:
A collapsible sidebar that narrates events in plain English as they happen — the foundation for LLM-powered shift handover summaries.

Demo Sequence Controls:
A hidden control panel (bottom of page) with three buttons: Normal Operation → Trigger Cascade → OperatorAI Action. Designed for live judging demos.
